About Us

Akshaypatra Producer Company Limited was formed in the context of these perspectives. It’s a dynamic organization of enthusiastic and energetic, duty-bound people dedicated to the overall development of rural people and farmers. Many eminent bio scientists, agricultural scientists and social workers are associated with the company as consultants.

Encouraging farmers and making them inclined towards the safe and beneficial farming by promoting organic farming is the top priority of the company. After conducting a district wise survey in the state of U.P., company has initiated cultivation of food grains, fruits such as papaya and banana, flowers mainly variety of roses and marigold, vegetables, mushroom, dairy project and production of organic manure as its first step in this direction. Company also provides compost, high yielding seeds, pesticides and agricultural equipments, and is in the process of establishing nursery of fruits, flowers, and vegetables, all under one roof through its ambitious project, ‘Akshaypatra Agromart’. Company has already opened its first Agromart in Itaunja, Lucknow.

Company follows a strict. ‘no chemical fertilizers or insecticides’ policy.

Our Vision

The vision of company is to create opportunities of self employment in the villages. Company also aims to open ‘e-mandi’ to ensure the best rates for the produce of farmers, by March 2018 in addition to:

  • Developing entrepreneurship and strengthening rural economy by working towards making rural areas progress economically and socially
  • Encouraging agriculture based industries, food processing and milk production units and providing employment opportunities to villagers and farmers in their respective areas.

Our Mission

  • Providing technical assistance for encouraging organic farming and necessary resources in order to obtain safe, nutritious, and beneficial food ingredients.
  • Providing information about climate change and new agricultural technology, organizing choupal, kisan goshthi, and kisam mela etc. regarding research and technical development.
  • Ensuring the availability of organic compost, fertilizers, and top quality seeds under one roof through ‘Akshaypatra Agromart’.
  • Ensuring availability of advance seeds, fruits, flowers, and vegetables to farmers on subsidized rates.
  • Encouraging farmers for profitable farming and cultivation by forming self-help groups and farmer’s club.
  • Providing suggestions regarding the seeding and maintenance of crop by proper soil testing and solutions.
  • Providing a fare market to farmers in local and remote province through ‘e-mandi’.

Objectives

  • To carry on the business of production, harvesting, procurement, grading, pooling, handling, marketing, selling of all agriculture and medical produce of the members and to promote all activities in relation with organic and medical farming and to promote agriculture activities, development and marketing of agricultural products, encourage, assist, and extend knowledge and information connected with production, marketing, recessing of agriculture reduce either through lectures, seminar, workshop by holding exhibitions and conferences.
  • To carry out business and exhibition and detailed research in the field of knowledge relating with the agriculture and organic farming, production of medicinal plants and herbs.
  • To carry out research and development in the field of organic farming, animal husbandry, gardening, floriculture, horticulture, and medical farming.
  • To produce all types of products related to horticulture, floriculture, viticulture, forestry, forest products, dairy products, vegetation, rising and farming plantation products and sell it to the market.
