About Us

Akshaypatra Producer Company Limited was formed in the context of these perspectives. It’s a dynamic organization of enthusiastic and energetic, duty-bound people dedicated to the overall development of rural people and farmers. Many eminent bio scientists, agricultural scientists and social workers are associated with the company as consultants.

Encouraging farmers and making them inclined towards the safe and beneficial farming by promoting organic farming is the top priority of the company. After conducting a district wise survey in the state of U.P., company has initiated cultivation of food grains, fruits such as papaya and banana, flowers mainly variety of roses and marigold, vegetables, mushroom, dairy project and production of organic manure as its first step in this direction. Company also provides compost, high yielding seeds, pesticides and agricultural equipments, and is in the process of establishing nursery of fruits, flowers, and vegetables, all under one roof through its ambitious project, ‘Akshaypatra Agromart’. Company has already opened its first Agromart in Itaunja, Lucknow.

Company follows a strict. ‘no chemical fertilizers or insecticides’ policy.
