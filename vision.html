Our Vision

The vision of company is to create opportunities of self employment in the villages. Company also aims to open ‘e-mandi’ to ensure the best rates for the produce of farmers, by March 2018 in addition to:

  • Developing entrepreneurship and strengthening rural economy by working towards making rural areas progress economically and socially
  • Encouraging agriculture based industries, food processing and milk production units and providing employment opportunities to villagers and farmers in their respective areas.
